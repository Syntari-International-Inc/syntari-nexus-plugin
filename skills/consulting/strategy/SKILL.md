# Strategy Consulting Frameworks Hub

9 strategy frameworks from McKinsey, BCG, Bain, and Deloitte for organizational assessment, portfolio analysis, and structured problem-solving.

> Before executing, read `../../_shared/plugin-context.md` for Nexus plugin conventions.

## Prerequisites

- **Nexus MCP** (optional): `nexus_list_portfolio`, `nexus_get_deal` for company/deal data
- **BigData.com MCP** (optional): `bigdata_company_tearsheet` for market and financial context
- **Manual fallback**: Provide company data, market context, and strategic questions directly

## Framework Registry

| Framework | Origin | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **7-S Alignment** | McKinsey | Diagnose alignment across strategy, structure, systems, values, skills, style, staff |
| **Three Horizons** | McKinsey | Map growth initiatives to H1 (defend core), H2 (build emerging), H3 (seed future) |
| **Nine-Box Matrix** | GE-McKinsey | Plot portfolio companies on 3x3 grid of attractiveness vs. strength |
| **Profit Pool** | McKinsey | Map where profit concentrates across the value chain |
| **Growth-Share Matrix** | BCG | Classify business units by market growth vs. relative market share |
| **Strategic Choice Cascade** | Deloitte/Monitor | 5 sequential strategy questions: aspiration, where to play, how to win, capabilities, systems |
| **Issue Tree** | McKinsey | Build MECE (mutually exclusive, collectively exhaustive) problem decomposition |
| **Storyline Builder** | McKinsey | Create pyramid-structured narratives with situation-complication-resolution |
| **Management Toolkit** | Bain | Score 25 tools by fit and produce implementation recommendations |

## When to Use Each

- **Organizational assessment**: 7-S Alignment -- identifies misalignment between strategy and execution
- **Growth planning**: Three Horizons -- prioritizes initiatives by time horizon
- **Portfolio decisions**: Nine-Box Matrix OR Growth-Share Matrix -- visual portfolio classification
- **Value chain analysis**: Profit Pool -- find where value is created and captured
- **Strategy formulation**: Strategic Choice Cascade -- systematic strategy design
- **Problem structuring**: Issue Tree -- break any complex problem into testable hypotheses
- **Communication**: Storyline Builder -- structure findings for executive audiences
- **Framework selection**: Management Toolkit -- when unsure which framework to apply

## Common Workflow

Issue Tree (structure problem) --> relevant framework (analyze) --> Storyline Builder (present findings)

## Seven-S Quick Reference

| Element | Type | Key Question |
|---------|------|-------------|
| Strategy | Hard | What is the plan to build competitive advantage? |
| Structure | Hard | How is the organization divided? |
| Systems | Hard | What are the daily procedures and processes? |
| Shared Values | Soft | What are the core beliefs and attitudes? |
| Skills | Soft | What competencies does the organization excel at? |
| Style | Soft | What is the leadership and management approach? |
| Staff | Soft | What are the workforce demographics and capabilities? |

## Three Horizons Quick Reference

| Horizon | Timeframe | Focus | Metric |
|---------|-----------|-------|--------|
| H1 | 0-12 months | Defend and extend core business | Revenue, margin |
| H2 | 12-36 months | Build emerging opportunities | Growth rate, market share |
| H3 | 36-72 months | Seed future options | Optionality value, experiments |

## Growth-Share Matrix Classification

| Quadrant | Market Growth | Relative Share | Action |
|----------|--------------|----------------|--------|
| Stars | High | High | Invest for dominance |
| Cash Cows | Low | High | Harvest cash flow |
| Question Marks | High | Low | Invest selectively or divest |
| Dogs | Low | Low | Divest or liquidate |
